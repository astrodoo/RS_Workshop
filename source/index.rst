ITS-RS Workshop Series at the University of Iowa
================================================


Introduction
~~~~~~~~~~~~

    ITS offers regular workshops on Linux and High-performance Computing (HPC) using the supercomputing cluster, Argon. This page provides supplementary materials for the workshops. Upcoming workshops will be announced on the `ITS-Research Services Workshops <https://research.its.uiowa.edu/workshops>`_. For detailed information about Argon, please visit our `Argon wiki page <https://uiowa.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/hpcdocs/pages/76513466/Argon+Cluster>`_.

    To access Argon, you will need to apply for an Argon account. If you do not have it yet, please visit our `Argon page <https://research.its.uiowa.edu/our-services/computing-services/argon-high-performance-computing-hpc>`_ to request access. Argon is the HPC system supported by the University of Iowa. Accounts on the system are available at **no cost to UI faculty, staff, and students** conducting research.

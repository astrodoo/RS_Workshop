ITS-RS Workshop Series at the University of Iowa¶

Introduction¶

ITS offers regular workshops on Linux and High-performance Computing (HPC) using the supercomputing cluster, Argon. This page provides supplementary materials for the workshops. Upcoming workshops will be announced on the ITS-Research Services Workshops. For detailed information about Argon, please visit our Argon wiki page.

To access Argon, you will need to apply for an Argon account. If you do not have it yet, please visit our Argon page to request access. Argon is the HPC system supported by the University of Iowa. Accounts on the system are available at no cost to UI faculty, staff, and students conducting research.
